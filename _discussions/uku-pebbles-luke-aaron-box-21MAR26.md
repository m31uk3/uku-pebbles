We require an interspecies (Agents + Humans) “shared caching layer”.

The Problem

When a human captures a screenshot, photo, or note, the surrounding context is often the most valuable information— why you saved it, what you were doing, how you felt, what you meant to do next.

Every capture tool today stores what you saw but discards how you experienced it. There is no standard format or process for preserving that ontology.

A “universal knowledge unit”.
